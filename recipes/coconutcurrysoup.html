Coconut Curry Soup

Ingredients

  • 1 can of evaporated coconut milk
  • Half of a jalapeno
  • 1 cup of kale
  • 1 cup of cabbage
  • Half of a yellow onion
  • Half a cup of cilantro
  • 1 tablespoon of butter or ghee
  • 2 tablespoons of coconut flour
  • 2-3 tablespoons of curry powder
  • 1 tablespoon of garlic powder or 2 cloves of garlic
  • 1-2 teaspoons of cayenne
  • 1 teaspoon of salt
  • 1 teaspoon of pepper
  • Half of a lime (juiced)
  • 2 chopped potatoes
  • 2 shredded chicken breasts
  • Half of a cup of green onions

Instructions

  1. Cook it all up
  2. Enjoy!
